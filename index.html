Dashboard · Power BI
P&L Dashboard
Executive financial dashboard built for CFO-level reporting. Tracks revenue, margins and cost performance against forecast and last year in real time, with drill-down by account, product, country, continent and date, from the consolidated P&L down to individual cost centers. Built on a dataset with randomly generated values for demonstration purposes.
Power BIDAXProfit and LossPower QueryFinancial Modeling

🎯 Objective

To centralize P&L reporting into a single interactive tool that lets finance leadership monitor revenue, cost and margin performance against both Forecast and Last Year in real time, replacing static Excel-based monthly closes with a live, drillable financial view.

👥 Target Audience

  • CFO: executive view with revenue, margin and EBIT KPIs, P&L waterfall and country-level risk ranking
  • Finance Manager: full P&L detail across Current Month, YTD and Full Year Estimate, filterable by country and product
  • Controller: variable and operating cost breakdown vs. forecast, down to individual account level

📊 Key Insights

  • Revenue grew 13.9% vs Forecast, but EBIT jumped 64.5%, the real story isn't top-line growth, it's SG&A discipline driving most of the upside
  • GP Margin is nearly flat vs Forecast (+0.2pp), confirming margin held steady while cost control did the heavy lifting on profit
  • 3 of the Top 5 countries are flagged "At Risk": Canada and Turkey are down vs. both LY and Forecast, with Turkey the most critical at -6.2% on both fronts
  • Variable costs spiked in June, landing 58.8% above forecast, the sharpest monthly deviation of the year

Python · Machine Learning
Customer Churn Prediction
End-to-end churn prediction project on the IBM Telco Customer dataset. Covers exploratory data analysis, feature engineering and comparison of 3 classification models: Logistic Regression, Random Forest and XGBoost. Random Forest was selected based on Recall as the priority metric for retention use cases.
PythonMachine Learningscikit-learnXGBoostPandasClassificationEDA

🎯 Objective

To predict which customers are at risk of churning before they cancel, enabling the retention team to act proactively and reduce revenue loss from preventable cancellations.

👥 Target Audience

  • Retention team: ranked list of at-risk customers to contact before they leave
  • Data team: model comparison, feature importance and methodology
  • Leadership: key churn drivers to inform pricing and contract strategy

📊 Key Insights

  • Month-to-month contracts churn at 42.7% vs 2.8% for two-year contracts, the strongest single predictor
  • Random Forest achieved 79.1% Recall, detecting 8 out of every 10 customers who actually churn
  • Tenure, monthly charges and contract type are the top 3 most predictive features, consistent with EDA findings
  • Electronic check payment method shows 45.3% churn rate vs 15% for automatic payment methods
